Kaptron: High-Precision Manufacturing Since 2009

Founded in 2009, Kaptron Plastics & Metals has established itself as a reliable partner for high-precision manufacturing. Located in the industrial hub of Punjab, India, we specialize in delivering export-quality mechanical components to global markets.

Core Machining Expertise

CNC Milling

Advanced 3-axis and 4-axis machining for complex geometries and tight tolerances.

CNC Turning

Precision turning for cylindrical components, ensuring superior surface finish and concentricity.

EDM Services

In-house Die-sinking EDM for intricate molds and cavities. Wire EDM managed through certified partners.

Die & Mould Manufacturing

Design and manufacturing of high-precision plastic injection moulds, die-casting dies, and custom tooling for a wide range of industries.

Pressure Die Casting Tooling

End-to-end development of pressure die casting moulds, including tool design, machining, EDM, fitting, and validation.

Injection Molding & Materials

Production-ready injection moulding support, including mould trials, polymer selection, and supply of moulded components.

We handle both ferrous and non-ferrous metals with equal expertise.

Materials We Master

  • Standard Metals: Aluminium Alloys, Mild Steel, Brass, Copper.
  • Hardened Metals: Stainless Steel (304, 316), Tool Steel (D2, P20, H13 etc.).
  • Cast Iron Components: Machining and finishing solutions for gray cast iron and ductile iron components.
  • Engineering Polymers: Delrin, Nylon, Peek, ABS, Polycarbonate, and other industrial polymers.

Industries Served

  • Medical Devices: High-precision components for surgical microscopes and medical instrumentation.
  • Agricultural Machinery: Durable parts for vegetable processing equipment and agricultural machinery.
  • Automotive & Tooling: Tooling, die casting dies, injection moulds, and precision-machined components for automotive suppliers.
