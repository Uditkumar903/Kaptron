Kaptron: High-Precision Manufacturing Since 2009

Founded in 2009, Kaptron Plastics & Metals has established itself as a reliable partner for high-precision manufacturing. Located in the industrial hub of Punjab, India, we specialize in delivering export-quality mechanical components to global markets.

Core Machining Expertise

CNC Milling

Advanced 3-axis and 4-axis machining for complex geometries and tight tolerances.

CNC Turning

Precision turning for cylindrical components, ensuring superior surface finish and concentricity.

EDM Services

In-house Die-sinking EDM for intricate molds and cavities. Wire EDM managed through certified partners.

We handle both ferrous and non-ferrous metals with equal expertise.

Materials We Master

  • Standard Metals: Aluminium Alloys, Mild Steel, Brass, Copper.
  • Hardened Metals: Stainless Steel (304, 316, etc.), Tool Steel (D2, P20, etc.).
  • Engineering Polymers: Delrin, Nylon, Peek, etc.

Industries Served

  • Medical Devices: We supply high-precision mechanical components for surgical microscopes to LABOMED.
  • Agricultural Machinery: We deliver robust vegetable processing equipment components to ALLROUND.

Rigorous Quality Control and Metrology

Quality is our foundation. We maintain a rigorous quality control process throughout the manufacturing cycle to ensure every component meets the exacting standards of our global clientele.
