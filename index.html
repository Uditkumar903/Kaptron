Derabassi, Punjab   |   Kaptron Plastics & Metals

We are looking for a fresher or experienced CNC professional who can manage production on Turning Centres, VMC, and HMC machines along with quality checks and basic troubleshooting.

  • Performing quality checks (Vernier, Micrometer, etc.).
